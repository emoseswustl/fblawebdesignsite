Custom Built PCs

Find more info about our PC build process, from start to finish.

The Build Process

There are two main factors that will affect what parts we use: what the PC will be used for and your budget. Gaming PCs will require higher-end components, while one for simple office applications will not. After determining that, we will work with you to create a parts list. These can include ones from our inventory or other sources. Some items may be out of stock due to the current chip shortage, so any in-demand parts will be swapped out with what's available before we finalize your parts list. We can also install the latest version of Windows or any other operating system upon request.


After your parts list is finished, we will order all parts and pick the ones needed from our inventory. A date will be scheduled for your PC to be built by one of our technicians. It could be the same day, or later depending on our schedule and part availability. Once that starts, most PCs will be available for pickup within 2 business days. Our build fee will be under $100, but it fluctuates depending on complexity. You will receive an exact quote once your parts list is finalized.


Once your PC is ready for pickup, we'll pack it up securely, along with any cables and accessories that came with the components. We fully test all PCs before sending them out, seeing whether they boot successfully and show all the attached components.
